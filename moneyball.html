Welcome to the World of Moneyball (2011)

Introduction:

Welcome to the official website of "Moneyball," the 2011 sports drama film directed by Bennett Miller, based on Michael Lewis's book "Moneyball: The Art of Winning an Unfair Game." Dive into the world of baseball strategy, statistics, and the incredible true story of how one team challenged tradition to revolutionize the game.

Synopsis:

"Moneyball" tells the story of Billy Beane, the general manager of the Oakland Athletics baseball team, who defies conventional wisdom and challenges the system by using computer-generated analysis to draft his players. With the help of statistician Peter Brand, Beane selects undervalued players based on their on-base percentage rather than traditional scouting methods. As the A's embark on an unprecedented winning streak, they defy all odds and capture the attention of the baseball world.

Key Cast:

  • Brad Pitt as Billy Beane
  • Jonah Hill as Peter Brand
  • Philip Seymour Hoffman as Art Howe
  • Robin Wright as Sharon
  • Chris Pratt as Scott Hatteberg
  • Kerris Dorsey as Casey Beane
